Is a strategy and innovation trainer forcorporates and entrepreneurs.

Has co-authored the book ‘In the Arena’ along with 14 other people from across the world.

Entrepreneurship is analogous to steering through a new territory without a map. How do we find our place in this new universe? Where do we start? When do we finish? How do we maneuver this space? You get into this space with a deep commitment to our idea, our mission and our desire to explore. What if you find a figuring instrument that can help you sense your reality better? What if you get a hold of maps and models for this difficult terrain? What if this instrument holds the knowledge and experiences of many entrepreneurs, not just one?

That’s exactly what ‘In the Arena – Unleash your Entrepreneurial Spirit, Make your Idea a Roaring Success’ is about. It’s co-authored by 15 entrepreneurs from 9 countries, where the diversity it brings adds to its richness. Click to buy this book.
